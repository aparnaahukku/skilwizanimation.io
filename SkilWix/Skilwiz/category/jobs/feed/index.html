]]> We are currently looking for Character Artists to join our Assets team onsite in our waterfront studio in Rhodes, Sydney.

This is a project-based contract.
Start ASAP.


Responsibilities:

  • Use artistic and technical skills to work on 3D CG characters
  • Modelling and texturing

  • Ability to work unassisted and relied upon to finish tasks
  • Be aware of project/task schedules and be capable of delivering work to deadlines
  • Engage with our  Asset Lead, Creative Director and Producers


Job requirements:

  • Juniors MUST have the equivalent of 1 solid year (minimum) professional experience working on feature films, AAA+ game titles, tv series and/or tvc’s.
  • Highly skilled using Z-Brush, Photoshop, 3DSMax and/or Maya
  • Detail oriented modelling
  • Proven expertise in both organic and hard surface matter
  • Ability to take direction and work well within a team focused environment
  • A solid understanding of real-world materials is essential and must be demonstrated in your portfolio/reel
  • Very strong artistic skills with a strong understanding of facial features and full body anatomy
  • Ability to evaluate time requirements and possess problem-solving skills
  • Positive, pro-active and excellent communication skills
  • Must have the right to work in Australia


Pluses:

  • Vray and/or Redshift expertise will be highly regarded
  • Those with Ornatrix experience will be fast-tracked in the application process
  • Experience with Marvelous Designer and/or Substance
  • Experience with Unreal Engine and creating models for In-engine use, etc
  • Experience leading and/or mentoring
  • Ability to light and render 3D objects
  • A traditional arts background with emphasis on sculpting
  • TD skills
